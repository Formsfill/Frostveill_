🏰 Base & Protection Guide

Base & Protection Strategies

Build underground, inside mountains, or under oceans to stay unnoticed.

Dig deep bases with secret entrances and multiple escape tunnels.

Build high in the sky and hide the access point to avoid raids.

Use obsidian layers, traps, and hidden storage to reduce losses.

Create TNT traps, piston traps, and fall traps to defend your base.

Hide loot under furnaces, inside walls, or underwater.

Blend your base with the biome using matching blocks.

Split loot into multiple hidden chests to avoid total loss.

Create a fake base with low-value loot to distract raiders.

Mountains, jungles, and oceans are harder to raid and easier to hide in.
