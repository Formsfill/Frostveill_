💡 Lifesteal SMP Crafting Tips

Learn essential crafting recipes for survival and PvP in FrostVeil Lifesteal SMP!

🛠️ Crafting Recipes Guide

Use 6 Wood Planks + 1 Iron Ingot to craft a Shield. Blocks arrows and reduces PvP damage.

Craft Iron Pickaxe before armor to mine diamonds faster and prepare for PvP.

3 Sticks + 1 Coal + 3 Logs = Campfire. Cooks food without fuel, perfect for survival.

Blaze Rod + 3 Cobblestone. Needed for Strength, Speed, and Regen potions in Lifesteal.

Craft Helmet first if PvPing (extra head protection). Chestplate provides maximum defense.

Axes deal high damage and can disable shields briefly. Very effective in Lifesteal PvP.

3 Iron Blocks + 4 Iron Ingots. Combine enchantments and repair your gear efficiently.

8 Obsidian + 1 Eye of Ender. Safe storage for your items even if you die in PvP.

4 Sand + 5 Gunpowder. Use for base traps and defending against raiders in Lifesteal SMP.

4 Obsidian + 2 Diamonds + 1 Book. Essential for Sharpness, Protection, and PvP advantage.
