Lifesteal Tips & Strategies

When you kill a player, you receive their player head and earn xp.

Only fight when you have better armor, potions, and positioning advantage. Never risk hearts in unnecessary fights.

Always keep backup armor and tools in hidden storage. Never carry all your valuables during PvP.

Teaming up increases survival chances and helps you control territory.

Strength, Speed, and Regeneration potions can completely change fight outcomes.

Always carry ender pearls, blocks, and golden apples to escape dangerous fights.
